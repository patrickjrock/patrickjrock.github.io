Neuroradiology glossary of terms


  • Disruption: A disruption is a congenital abormality in a structure with normal development potential [2]. (see malformation)
  • Malformation: A malformation is a congenital abnormality in a structure with abnormal development as a result of a genetic defect [2]. (see disruption)
  • Pituitary neuroendocrine tumor (PitNET): The 5th edition WHO Classification of Endocrine and Neuroendocrine Tumours rechristened pituitary adenoma as PitNET [1].
