Anki style guide

Vocabulary cards

Vocabulary cards are used for remembering acronyms, eponyms, and definitions. The front and back consist of text. Some vocabulary examples include:
  • Define airspace disease.
  • What does MPRAGE stand for?
  • What is the eponymous name for a distal radius fracture>

Pathognomic images

Pathognomic images are special cases where the differential diagnosis only contains one item. Pseudolesions and artifacts can be thought of as findings where the differential diagnosis contains zero items, for example a skin fold on a CXR.

Differential diagnoses

The card front can be exlplicit, for example what is the differential diagnosis for groudnd glass opacities in the lungs, or image based. That is given a picture containing a finding produce a differential diagnosis. Ideally for all importand ddxs both types of cards will be produced.
