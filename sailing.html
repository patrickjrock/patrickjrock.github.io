Patrick Rock's Sailing Resume

Summary

I grew up sailing small boats on lakes in Texas and have been participating in charters since 2023. I enjoy cruising, traditional navigation, and knots.

  • Total sailing days: 29 (2 as skipper)
  • Total distance: 775 nautical miles
  • Boat types: Monohulls, 20–46 ft
  • Regions: Chesapeake Bay, Galveston Bay, Baltic Sea, Salish Sea

Organizations

  • Latrappe Creek Historical and Ecological Society
  • The Center for Wooden Boats, Seattle
  • American Sailing Association ID: 2024220135

Certifications

  • ASA 101, Keelboat Sailing: completed June 2024 with South Coast Sailing on a Catalina 22 (Fire Water) in Galveston Bay.
  • ASA 103, Basic Coastal Cruising: completed July 2024 with Puget Sound Sailing on a Tartan 37 (Ørn) in Puget Sound. Practiced man overboard recovery, mooring, and anchoring.
  • ASA 104, Bareboat Cruising: completed June 2025 with San Juan Sailing on a Jeanneau 45 (Misty Blue Yonder) in the San Juan Islands. Practiced paper chart navigation and rigged a preventer.

Charter Experience

  • June 11, 15, 22, 2023

    Day-sailed as skipper on Fire Water, a Catalina 22, over 3 days (50 nautical miles) in Galveston Bay with Nate Shane out of Seabrook.

Highlights: Gennaker sailing, 60 nm overnight passage, and repairing a leak in the freshwater system. Ports of call: Warnemünde, Burgtiefe, Spodsbjerg, Nyborg, Ballen, Sejerø, Hundested, Helsingør, Copenhagen, Klintholmhavn.
