Patrick J Rock, MD

Neuroradiologist

Biography

Dr. Patrick J. Rock is a fellowship-trained neuroradiologist practicing in Dallas, Texas. He earned his MD from Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center after completing a BS in Computer Science at Texas A&M University, and trained at Houston Methodist Hospital and the University of Washington. His academic interests include advanced neuroimaging and case-based radiologic research.
